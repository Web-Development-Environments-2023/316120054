About me

Well, I'm Morgan Freeman, and I've had a pretty successful career as an actor, producer, and narrator. I've been in a lot of movies, TV shows, and stage productions, and I've won some awards for my work. You might remember me as Red in "The Shawshank Redemption," or God in "Bruce Almighty." But, I'm not just about my career. I care about giving back, too. I co-founded the Grenada Relief Fund and support several charities focused on education, the environment, and healthcare. In my personal life, I've been married twice and have four children.
